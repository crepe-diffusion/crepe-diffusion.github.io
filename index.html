CREPE: Controlling Diffusion with Replica Exchange

Abstract

How can we steer diffusion models toward new objectives without retraining?

CREPE introduces replica exchange as a powerful alternative to heuristic guidance or Sequential Monte Carlo for inference-time control. CREPE runs multiple controlled diffusion processes in parallel and exchanges information between them—achieving stable control, high diversity, and flexible trade-offs at generation time.

CREPE supports principled control, online refinement, and diverse sample generation, and works seamlessly across tasks such as temperature annealing (powering), reward tilting, model composition, and classifier-free guidance debiasing. CREPE also applies to masked diffusion or continuous diffusions with a unified framework.
